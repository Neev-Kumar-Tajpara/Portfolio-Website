# Design System: Neev Portfolio (Institutional Motion)
**Project ID:** TBD

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**Vibe:** Institutional, Mathematical, Systematic, High-Signal.
**Philosophy:** "Experimental in motion, disciplined in layout."
**Aesthetic:** Brutalist but polished. Motion-driven but controlled. No clutter.
**Motion:** Smooth (Lenis), Parallax-aware, Section-based transitions.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
### Primary (Section 1 - Default)
*   **Void Black (#050505)**: Primary background. Deep, infinite.
*   **Electric Pink (#FF0080)**: Primary typography and accents. High contrast, bold, mathematical signal.
*   **Grid Grey (#262626)**: Subtle geometric grid lines.

### Secondary (Section 2 - Inversion)
*   **Electric Pink (#FF0080)**: Background flood.
*   **Charcoal (#121212)**: Primary text. High contrast against pink.
*   **Soft White (#FAFAFA)**: Secondary accents.

### Tertiary (Section 3 - Institutional)
*   **Deep Navy (#0A192F)**: Background. Serious, trustworthy.
*   **Clinical White (#FFFFFF)**: Minimal typography.
*   **Data Blue (#64FFDA)**: Chart elements and dynamic data.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Font Family**: `Inter` (sans-serif) for UI/Layout. `JetBrains Mono` (monospace) for data, code, and technical labels.
*   **Headings**: Large, tracking-tight, uppercase (e.g., "SYSTEMATIC ALPHA").
*   **Body**: Clean, high-readability, generous leading.
*   **Mono**: Used for "System Status", timestamps, tickers, and footer metadata.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons**: Sharp corners (radius-none), 1px borders. Hover: Inverse color swap (fill).
*   **Cards**: Minimalist. 1px border (#333). No shadows (flat). Hover: Subtle Y-axis lift + border highlight.
*   **Inputs**: Underlined or raw boxes. Monospace text.
*   **Grid**: Visible 1px grid lines separating sections.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Grid**: 12-column mathematical grid. Visible lines encouraged.
*   **Spacing**: Generous whitespace (padding-y-24). "Museum-style" isolation of elements.
*   **Scroll**: Sections fill viewport (min-h-screen). content reveals on scroll.
